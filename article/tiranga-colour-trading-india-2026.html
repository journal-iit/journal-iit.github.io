How Colour Trading Works on Tiranga

The mechanics are deliberately simple. Each round opens with a countdown — typically 60 to 180 seconds. You select one of three outcomes: Red, Green, or Violet. When the timer hits zero, the result is announced. If your prediction matches, the payout multiplier is applied to your trade amount and the winnings land in your wallet immediately. No waiting, no batching, no manual withdrawal triggers.

What makes Tiranga feel different from a lot of prediction apps is that the result announcement is clean and verifiable. Every outcome is logged in your game history with a timestamp. Several users I spoke to mentioned this specifically — "you can go back and check every single round." That kind of transparency is rare and it matters enormously in a market where trust in gaming platforms is earned slowly.
